Ian St. Clair

Ian got his jeans from Berlin, and loved them for many years. They were one of the first pairs of mens jeans he bought, and have important meaning to him and his journey. After many years of wearing them, Ian’s jeans started to fall apart. There are holes on the inner thighs, and he can no longer wear them. They’re a sentimental piece for him, and he sent them to Rewoven to be made wearable and fashionable for him again.

Mei’s jeans are a hand me down from her girlfriend. Though they fit her well around the waist, she’s always found them too long and was never able to wear them. She wants to practice more sustainable fashion, so rather than throwing away the jeans she sent them to Rewoven to be repaired and customized.
