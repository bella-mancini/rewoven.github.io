How it works

lightbulb Get inspired

Whether it's looking at our happy customers page or finding inspiration on pinterest, get an idea of what you would want your pants to be transformed into.

Send Submit Form

After looking at some inspiration fill out the form with the how you want your pants to look.

mail Check Email

When you submit your form you will recieve an email with next steps, timeline, and a shipping tag to put on your package so you can send it to us.

local_shipping Ship pants

Using the shipping label in your email send us your package. When it is recieve you will get an email letting you know we are working on your order.

gesture Pants get rewoven

Check out the Jessica's process page to see all the care and thought that is put into your pants.

memory NFC tag added

As the final touches are put on your pants we add a water proof NFC tag to the back. This tag is similar to a QR code as it's not connected to the internet, but when it's tapped with a phone our website will pop up!

local_shipping Pants is shipped back

When your pants are ready to go you'll recieve an email letting you know they are on their way. Get ready to see your new statement piece in 5-10 business days.

package Pants arrive

The day has finally come try on your pants and scan the NFC tag to learn more about your pants!
